Mission Statement

This website is aimed to spotlight and champion smaller-level LGBT artists that are musicking in the current music scene.

I believe that artists that are either LGBT or Pro-LGBT are not given the correct opportunities or fair chance in the music industry to grow effectively.

This website aims to properly give them this chance.

As artists, we are often bound by the shackles of the culture surrounding us - from genre, field, and those that shape contemporary music society.

By removing these shackles, you find a wave of artists who are consistently endeveaoured to create better sonic experiences than those before them.

With a condense and finite-sized simple indexed resource, we are able to fairly spotlight artists who are deserving of a chance. This will allow us to create a future where representation is a higher matter.
