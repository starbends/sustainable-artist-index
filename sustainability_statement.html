Sustainability Statement

This website is built upon a fairly inline-based code structure, to improve streaming performance, reduce file size, and make less calls to the server, which is lowering

carbon emissions in the process.

I am also running this website on a more eco-friendly hosting provider, which utilises virtual servers to cut down on electrical energy, as well as

being an inland company, based in the UK to reduce the use of outsourced materials and trade.

-

I accept donations to keep this website alive, however I do not accept crypto. This is due to crypto being a heavily energy-waste-heavy format

of financial gain; due to mining server farms.

-

I have used no imagery as to reduce data traffic going inbound/outbound of the server. I'm utilising proper caching where appropriate via my host's panel

and utilising features to stop bot-crawlers on the website; in the name of affordable energy.

-

This website was also built with help and hints from people of different genders, ethnicities, sexualities, and cultures. I aim to support the gender-equality acts

wherever possible, as well as adhering to the global sustainable development goals.

-

Wherever possible, this website aims to inform, teach, and educate people on musical practices, cultures, freedoms of expression, and the allowance of better performance as a creative.

Pages will include works from people of all races, genders, ages, cultures, ethnicities etc wherever appropriate.
